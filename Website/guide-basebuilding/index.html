Base Building

Making a base is important for shelter and to store your resources and workbenches, as well as various other things. Hold Q to open a menu, and click 'Props' to spawn props and 'Structures' to spawn structures which will help you make tools to progress.

Torch

Workbench Menu

A Torch is a useful tool which will act a light source whether you hold it or place it in your home. To make a torch, go to a crafting workbench, find the torch, and left click on it. (Torch is on the bottom right in the picture). When you have made the torch, go to your 'Tools' tab in the Q menu and click the torch. This will make you pull it out. You can then place it on the ground and pick it back up as well, making it a good light source for a base.

Structures

To make structures, hold Q and go to the 'Structures' tab. To begin with, I recommend making the workbench first, to be able to make tools. If you need more construction level, Make some props first. (Make a base)

Making a workbench

After the workbench has been placed, move it where you would like it to be, and press USE on it to open a menu where you can craft tools.

Crafting Menu

Props

Prop Menu

To make props, hold Q and go to the 'Props' tab. From here, you can build any prop you like, providing you have the materials. Building props increases your construction XP.

A simple base

I recommend equipping the physics gun to your hotbar to move the props about to make your base. You can find doors under structures, which will open/close when you press USE on them.
