Basing Rules

Don't block resources!

Don't cover or otherwise block/prevent other players from accessing natural resources like stone/ores. This includes NPCs like Vorty and Breen.

No basing in arena, or outside the map!

Don't build bases in the arena, or (if you somehow get out there) in the void of which is outside the map boundries.

'Do Not Enter' Signs

If a player has a sign along the lines of 'Do not enter' on their entrance(s) to their base, if you're not in PvP then you're not allowed to enter unless they give you permission. If they ask you to leave, you must leave, unless you're in PvP.

Play Fair!

Abide by the other rules in Base Rules and PvP Rules as well as these. No finding loopholes, or generally being a nuisance.
