Main server Rules

English only!

Speak English in the default voice and text channel and no spam in text or voice either please! You can speak any language and spam all you like in your own voice channel. Hold Q and go to 'voice channels' to make your own.

!unstuck only when stuck

Do not use !unstuck to abuse game mechanics for example PvP!

No Hacking!

No hacking or cheating or exploiting, in any way. This includes abusing game mechanics to gain an unfair benefit, and macros.

False Accusations

No false accusations to get people into trouble!

No harassment, spamming, or trolling!

Be respectful of people and use common sense, no derogatory language e.g racism or sexism.

No Advertising!

Do not advertise other servers on this server.

No Illegal activity

No talking about anything illegal, in voice or text chat. This includes DDOS and DOS threats, and talking about scripts/cheating.

No Prop Abuse

Do not use props to gain an unfair advantage in the game or to block, push, or generally abuse props. Do not use Entities / props to gain advantage on any NPCs. No building props/entities in arena or using props to gain an advantage in arena.

Other people's buildings

If you're not in PvP and they ask you to leave, you must leave! Unless all people involved are in PvP, then this is counted as a raid. Plants inside people's buildings are owned by them.

Admins' Word

Admins' Word is final! Finding loopholes in the rules and generally causing trouble for other players and getting reports can get you punished. Basically, don't be a dick.

Portals

Don't prevent other players from being able to use a portal purposefully, by blocking them, dialling the button to stop theirs, or generally prevent it from being used in other ways - This also applies in PvP

Play Fair!

Abide by the other rules in Base Rules and PvP Rules as well as these. No finding loopholes, or generally being a nuisance.

Racism/Sexism/etc

Not allowed.
