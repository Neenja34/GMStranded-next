Quick Start

The info below will be enough to get you started, the tabs up the top or on the right will bring you to a page with a little more detail on the topic! You can view the server's rules here!

E (USE key)

Your 'E' key is used for most processes, like cutting trees, drinking water, eating berries and mining.

Collected resources

Once you have collected resources you can go into the Q menu to find them. It's recommended to get a workbench first which will be in structures, and plant some seeds, which will be in the farming tab.

The workbench

When pressing E on a structure like a workbench, it brings up a menu and from here you'll be able to make tools, like a fishing rod or a pickaxe/hatchet.

The hotbar

Pressing F2 will bring up the hotbar. If you hold down Q, and then drag tools from your Q menu onto the hotbar, you can then use 1-9 on the keyboard to quickly change weapon.

Fishing

At the workbench, it is possible to make a fishing rod that you can equip. Holding it out, and then left clicking on water will start the process. This will give you a raw fish.

Cooking on a stove

To make a stove, go to structures in the Q menu and make one with the required resources. Press USE (E) on the stove and click on the fish you choose to cook. Wait until the pot turns to grey (If it goes black you've burnt it) and then press USE (E) on the pan.

Melon aids

Ocassionally, from eating melons you will get melon aids. You can make a cure for this at a furnace (located in structures) or brewing one in an alchemy lab. Melon aids will drain your health for a little while until it expires, or you die. You can't catch melon aids from fish.

Portals

If you're wondering about the portal in the middle of the spawn island, you can press USE (E) on the red button on the dialler next to the portal, and then double click on the location you want to teleport to. See the 'Stargate Portals' tab for more info.
