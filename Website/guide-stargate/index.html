Stargate Portals

Stargate Portal Picture

Stargate portals are the way to get from world to world. Different worlds have different resources and enemies, so exploring is typically a good idea! The portals look like the picture linked above.

How do I use them?

Dialler Pic

There should be a DHD (Dialler) Next to the portal. Press the Big red button on it, and double click on the world you want to go to. The portal will spin up and generate the iris for you to walk through.

Speed Dial (A.K.A WHY IS THE PORTAL SO DAMN SLOW!?)

Speed dial tick box

Tick the little box next to the address as shown in the picture below. This will turn on speed dial, which makes it much, MUCH faster.
