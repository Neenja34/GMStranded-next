Basic Needs

Basic Needs on HUD

Down the bottom left of the screen, you will see your health, hunger, thirst and fatigue.

(Health) - When your health reaches 0, you die. When hunger or thirst reaches 0, you start to lose health. When your fatigue reaches 0, you will pass out, which can in turn, damage your health if you are in the open.

(Hunger) - Eat berries (which you can get from farming) or cooked fish (which you can get from fishing, then cooking the fish on a stove) to regain the hunger meter.

Cooking Menu | | Fruit

(Thirst) - Go to a body of water and press USE to drink and regain the thirst meter.

Water

(Fatigue) - Go to a safe location i.e indoors, in your base, or on a bed, and type !sleep to sleep, and regain fatigue. Press USE on beds to sleep on them. Sleeping gains HP.

Melon Aids

Melon Aids can be caught from eating fruit. It will damage you over time, and you can tell when you have melon aids as a new icon will appear on your HUD, that looks like this:

Melon Aids will wear off by itself after some time, however you can make a cure in the Alchemy bench if you have the resources. It would be best to eat fish for the time being.

Cooking

Cooking Menu

Pressing USE on a Grill will open the cooking menu. Here you can cook fish, eggs, bread, meat, pies and pizzas! Providing you have the resources required to craft it. Click on the item you want to cook, and it will cook!

Farming

Farming requires seeds. To get seeds, press USE on the ground to forage (or left click with a hoe). When you have seeds, you need to open the Q menu, and go to the 'farming' tab. Look at the ground, and left click on the seed in your q menu to plant it. The seed will plant into the ground, and begin to grow. After a short while, it will have grown and produced fruit. Pressing USE on the fruit will eat it (with a risk of Melon Aids), or using a seed extractor on it will extract seeds from it, destroying the fruit.

Bottling Water

Bottling water gif

To bottle water, simply go to a body of water, go into your Q menu and go to the 'options' tab. From there, you can click the 'Bottle water' command.

Making an aid pack

In order to make an aid pack, you need to make a first aid workbench with 15 wood and gather 5 pypa seeds. Hold Q, go to structures and find the first aid workbench and make it.

Making a first aid workbench

When you have made the bench, place it where you want it and press USE on it. This will open a menu where you can craft the aid pack.
